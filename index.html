Trang Ha (b. 1996 VN) likes to play within the multidisciplinary. She considers art something constantly uncertain and doubtful, always in the seek for other perspectives. Although trained as a designer, she wants to incorporate artistic practices into her design, hoping to unveil the complexity of the con- and a- temporary of the ever expanding (art) world. Trang finished her BA Graphic Design at the Royal Academy of Art in The Hague (NL) in 2020.
